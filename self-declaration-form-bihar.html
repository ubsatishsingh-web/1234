Self Declaration Form Bihar Zameen 2026 | शपथ पत्र (Affidavit) Format और Guide

⚡ Important Notice

Self Declaration (Shapath Patra) ek zaroori document hai zameen se judi kai services ke liye.

Notary se banwayein — sirf ₹50-200 mein! Neeche Hindi format bhi diya gaya hai.

📜 Self Declaration Form kya hota hai?

Bihar mein zameen registry, mutation (Dakhil Kharij), LPC certificate, PM Awas Yojana aur bank loan jaise kaamo ke liye Self Declaration Form (शपथ पत्र / Affidavit) ek mukhya document mana jata hai.

  • Teen naam — ek hi cheez: Self Declaration Form, Shapath Patra (शपथ पत्र), aur Affidavit. Teeno ka matlab ek hi hota hai.
  • Sworn Statement: Yeh ek legal statement hota hai jisme aap khud declare aur swear karte hain ki di gayi information sach hai.
  • Notary verification: Yeh document Notary Public ya Judicial Magistrate ke saamne sign va certify kiya jaata hai.
  • Legal Responsibility: Affidavit ek legal document hai. Isme galat jaankari dena IPC Section 193 ke tahat dandniya apradh (punishable crime) hai.
📋

Kya hai?

Aapka signed sworn statement jo certify karta hai ki diya gaya information sach hai.

🏛️

Kaun banata hai?

Notary Public ya Judicial Magistrate ke saamne sign aur seal hota hai.

💰

Cost kitni hai?

Sirf ₹50-200 Notary fee lagti hai. Bahut sasta aur aasaan!

Standard Format

Aap is Hindi format ko A4 paper par print ya stamp paper par type karwa sakte hain:

🛠️ Shapath Patra Kaise Banwayein?

METHOD 1 (Recommended)

🏛️ Notary se Banwayein

⏱️ Time: Same day 💰 Cost: ₹50-200
  1. Upar diya format print karein ya typed copy lao
  2. Nearest Notary ke paas jayein (court ke paas milte hain)
  3. Apna ID proof saath le jayein (Aadhar Card)
  4. Notary ke saamne sign karein
  5. Notary seal aur sign karega
  6. Done! ₹50-200 fee dein
✅ Sabse legal aur reliable tareeka
METHOD 2 (Alternative)

✍️ Khud Likh Sakte Hain

Kuch simple cases mein Notary ki zaroorat nahi hoti. Aap safed kaagaz par plain self declaration likhkar signature kar sakte hain.

⚠️ Bank ya scheme office se pehle confirm karein ki plain self declaration chalega ya Notary seal zaroori hai.
⚡ Plain paper self-attestation
METHOD 3 (Online)

💻 Online Affidavit Services

Kuch online legal portals hain jaise LegalDocs, Vakilsearch wagairah jo online affidavit bana kar aapke ghar delivering karte hain.

Ghar baithe stamp paper par typing & notary delivery mil sakti hai, lekin extra cost lagti hai.
🌐 Home delivery option (Costlier)

📍 Notary Office Kahan Milega?

Bihar ke sabhi zilon mein Notary advocate in jagahon par milte hain:

  • 🏛️ District Court (जिला न्यायालय) ke paas — Main Location
  • 🏢 SDM Office / Sub-Divisional Court ke paas
  • 🏬 Tehsil / Block (प्रखंड) office ke aas paas
  • ⚖️ Advocate chambers aur typist counters par
🔍 Google Search Tip:
Google Map par type karein: "[Apna Jila] notary public" (Jaise: Patna notary public ya Gaya notary public).

Rural / Block Level Court ₹50 - ₹150
Online Legal Services (Home Delivery) ₹200 - ₹500
⏱️ Time needed: Notary wala same day mein banwa deta hai — sirf 30 minute ka kaam hai!

⚠️ Shapath Patra mein Dhyan Rakhein

⚠️ Warning 1: Galat Info Na Dein

Affidavit mein jhooth bolna ya galat jaankari dena IPC Section 193 ke under punishable crime hai. Isme jail aur fine dono ho sakta hai.

⚠️ Warning 2: Blank Affidavit Par Sign Na Karein

Kabhi bhi khali (blank) paper ya incomplete affidavit par sign na karein. Poori details fill aur check karne ke baad hi signature karein.

⚠️ Warning 3: Notary Seal Compulsory

Notary ka official stamp aur seal zaroori hai. Bina seal va Notary registration number ke affidavit valid nahi maana jaata.

✅ Pro Tip: Photocopies Rakh Lein

Affidavit submit karne se pehle iski 2-3 photocopies apne paas surakshit rakh lein. Original ko safe jagah se attach karein.
