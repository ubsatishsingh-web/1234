❓ FAQ — अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले सवाल (Bihar Land Cost FAQ)

Q: Bihar mein zameen kharidne mein total kitna kharcha aata hai?

▼

A: Bihar mein zameen kharidne par stamp duty male buyer ke liye 6% aur female ke liye 5.7% hoti hai. Iske sath 2% registration fee lagti hai. Iske alawa scanning fee, lawyer fee aur mutation fee milakar total property rate ka lagbhg 9% se 11% extra government aur professional charges ke roop mein kharcha aata hai.

Q: Stamp duty aur registry fee alag alag hoti hai?

▼

A: Haan, dono alag hain. Stamp Duty sarkar ka revenue tax hai jo legal agreement (Stamp) par lagta hai jo male ke liye 6% hai. Registration Fee Sub-Registrar Office (SRO) ka service charge hai jo court book entry ke liye flat 2% liya jata hai.

Q: Registration fee maximum kitni hoti hai Bihar mein?

▼

A: 2026 ke updated niyam ke mutabik Bihar mein registration fee maximum ₹60,000 par cap hoti hai. 2% calculate karne par agar fee 60,000 se upar jati hai, toh bhi buyer ko sirf ₹60,000 hi dena hoga.

Q: Female ke naam par registry mein kitni bachat hoti hai?

▼

A: Female ke naam par land property registry karne par stamp duty rate 5.7% lagta hai (Male ke liye 6%). Isse buyer ko direct property value par 0.3% ki cash saving milti hai. For example, 50 Lakh ki property par direct ₹15,000 ki bachat hogi.

Q: Agent ki fee kitni hoti hai Bihar mein?

▼

A: Bihar property market mein zameen agents ya brokers sadharan taur par zameen ki deal value par 1% se lekar 2% tak brokerage charge karte hain. Direct deal karne par aap is pure kharche ko bacha sakte hain.

Q: Sale deed draft karne mein kitna kharcha aata hai?

▼

A: Sale deed drafting fee lawyer aur deed writer par depend karti hai. Standard range ₹2,000 se ₹10,000 tak hoti hai. Agar aap direct sarkari portal se template download karke likhte hain toh sirf stamp duty ka cost dena padta hai.

Q: Dakhil Kharij aur LPC ki fees kitni hai?

▼

A: Dakhil Kharij (Mutation) ke liye online sarkari fee lagbhg ₹150 hoti hai (Online service charge ke sath). LPC (Land Possession Certificate) banwane ki normal processing fee flat ₹30-50 hai. Dono process online biharbhumi website se apply kiye jate hain.

Q: Home loan lene par extra kya kharcha hota hai?

▼

A: Bank se home loan lene par processing fee (0.5%), technical valuation fee (₹5,000-10,000), stamp and MODT charges, aur home loan default security property insurance policy (0.3%) ke charges extra judte hain.

Q: Stamp duty circle rate par lagti hai ya market rate par?

▼

A: Stamp duty registration hamesha "Circle Rate" (Minimum Valuation Rate - MVR) aur "Actual Deal Value" mein se jo bhi jada hoga, usi amount par calculate ki jati hai. MVR se kam rate par registry nahi ho sakti.

Q: Kya yeh charges negotiable hain?

▼

A: Sarkari charges jaise Stamp Duty aur Registration Fee bilkul fixed hote hain, inme koi negotiation nahi hota. Halanki, private costs jaise Lawyer Fee, Document writer, aur Property Brokerage charges highly negotiable hain.
