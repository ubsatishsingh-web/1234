Issue #1

🏚️ Padosi ne Zameen par Kabza Kar Liya — Kya Karein?

"Yeh bahut common aur dukhdayi situation hai Bihar mein. Par ghabrana nahi — kanoon aapke saath hai."

Step-by-Step Solution:

Step 1: Shant rahein — gusse mein koi kadam mat uthayein

"Pehle saari jankari ikattha karein." Ladayi-jhagde se aapka legal case kamzor ho sakta hai.

Step 2: Apne documents ikattha karein:
  • ✅ Bainama / Registry Copy (Kharid-bikri ki mool pratilipi)
  • ✅ Jamabandi / Khatyan Record
  • ✅ LPC (Land Possession Certificate)
  • ✅ Khata - Khesra Number details
  • ✅ Photos / Videos of Encroachment (Kabza kiye hue jagah ki tasveerein)
Step 3: Apna record verify karein online:

"Confirm karein ki aapka naam sarkari record mein hai:"

🌐 Portal Link: land.bihar.gov.in/Ror2025/RoR.aspx ↗
Step 4: Local resolution try karein:

"Pehle panchayat ya mukhiya se baat karein — aksar local level par hal ho jaata hai." Gram Katchahry mein bhi shikayat de sakte hain.

Step 5: Police Complaint:

"Naya Kabza hone par FIR darj karein Local thane mein — Section 145 CrPC." Agar aashanka ho toh Thane mein written आवेदन dein.

Step 6: SDM/DM ke paas application:

"Section 145/146 CrPC ke under SDM ke paas possession dispute file kar sakte hain." SDM stay order de sakta hai.

Step 7: Civil Court:

"Possession suit file karein Civil Court mein." "Lawyer zaroori hai yahan." Court se injunction (stray order) maangein.

Step 8: Jan Shikayat (Online Portal):

Bihar Rajswa Department ke official Public Grievance portal par online shikayat karein.

Online Shikayat Karein ↗
Issue #2

📄 Zameen Kagaz mein Galti — Kaise Theek Karein?

Agar aapki Jamabandi ya RoR record mein koi galti aa gayi hai, toh online sudhar karwana aasaan hai.

Common Types of Errors (Kagaz ki Galatiyan):

  • ❌ Naam ki spelling galat
  • ❌ Size / Raqba galat
  • ❌ Khata / Khesra number galat
  • ❌ Land type (Kism Zameen) galat

Parimarjan Portal Correction Solution

Bihar Sarkar ke official Parimarjan Plus portal se digital correction apply karein.

Parimarjan Portal ↗

Correction ke Complete Steps:

  1. Step 1: Portal par register / login karein (Mobile OTP se login).
  2. Step 2: "Apply for Correction" (परिमार्ज के लिए आवेदन) select karein.
  3. Step 3: Error type chunein (Spelling, Raqba, Khata Khesra, ya Dakhil Kharij error).
  4. Step 4: Sahi information bharein (Asli kagaz ke mutabiq).
  5. Step 5: Supporting documents upload karein (Registered Bainama, Purana Khatyan, Self-declaration).
  6. Step 6: Application Form submit karein aur Tracking ID note karein.
  7. Step 7: CO office (Anchal Circle Office) se verification hogi.
  8. Step 8: 30-60 din ke andar record mein online correction update ho jayega.
Issue #3

👨‍👩‍👧 Family mein Zameen Batwara Kaise Karein?

"Family property division ek sensitive matter hai. Sabse pehle aamne saamne baat karein."

3 Effective Methods of Partition:

Method 1 — Best Option

Mutual Agreement (आपसी सहमति)

"Sabhi family members baithein aur milkar decide karein. Ek partition deed banwayein lawyer se aur register karwayein."

⏱️ Time: 1-2 hafte
Method 2 — Panchayati

Mediation (मध्यस्थता)

"Koi trusted third party (mukhiya, elder, sarpanch) ko mediator banayein. Unke saamne agreement karein."

⏱️ Time: 1 mahina
Method 3 — Last Resort

Court Partition Suit

"Civil Court mein Partition Suit file karein.
Time: 2-5 saal
Cost: Lawyer fees + court fees"

⏱️ Time: 2-5 saal

Required Documents for Family Batwara:

  • ✅ Purana Bainama / Ancestral Khatyan
  • ✅ Current Jamabandi copy
  • ✅ Family tree / Vanshavali / Relationship proof
  • ✅ All family members Aadhaar / ID proof
Issue #4

💰 Seller ne Paisa Liya Par Zameen Nahi Di — Kya Karein?

"Yeh ek serious fraud hai. Turant action lena zaroori hai."

Immediate Steps to Take:

Step 1: Written demand notice bhejen (lawyer ke through legal notice bhejein).
Step 2: Police FIR darj karein — IPC Section 420 (Fraud) & Section 406 (Criminal Breach of Trust).
Step 3: Civil Court mein case file karein — "Specific Performance of Contract" (अनुबंध का पालन).
Step 4: Consumer Forum (agar builder ya developer ka Mamla hai).

Evidence Collect Karein:

  • ✅ Payment receipts / Cash vouchers
  • ✅ Mahadnama / Agreement to Sell copy
  • ✅ Witness statements (Gawahon ke bayan)
  • ✅ WhatsApp / SMS / Call recording proofs
  • ✅ Bank transfer / Cheque proof

⚠️ ALERT: Agle baar zameen kharidne se pehle:

  • ✅ Seller ka Khata-Khesra online verify karein
  • ✅ Registered bainama hi karein
  • ✅ Paise tab dein jab registry ho
  • ✅ Lawyer se documents check karwayein
Issue #5

🏛️ Sarkari Record mein Meri Zameen Doosre ke Naam Hai

Agar survey ya galti se aapki zameen ka Khata/Jamabandi kisi aur ke naam par bol raha hai, toh step-by-step sudhar karwayein:

  1. Step 1: Apne documents verify karein (Original Bainama, Purana Khatyan, Dakhil Kharij rasid).
  2. Step 2: Portal check karein: land.bihar.gov.in/Ror2025/RoR.aspx ↗
  3. Step 3: Parimarjan portal par online correction apply karein (Supporting proof ke saath).
  4. Step 4: Agar dono ke paas Jamabandi hai — Civil Court mein Title Suit (टाइटिल सूट) file karein.
  5. Step 5: Jan Shikayat portal par Shikayat darj karein.
🌐 Jan Shikayat Karein ↗
Issue #6

📏 Zameen ki Seema / Haddi ka Vivad (Boundary Dispute)

Aad/Haddi dabane ya padosi dwara seema aage badhane ke mamle mein yeh process follow karein:

Step 1: Bhu Naksha check karein:

"Khesra number se original boundaries dekhen." Official map official scale par dekhein.

Step 2: Fresh Survey Request:

Revenue Survey office / Anchal Amin dwara naapi (Boundary demarcation) ka aavedan dein.

Step 3: Field Verification:

Local Revenue Officer (Patwari / Halka Karamchari) se Mauke par jaanch karwayein.

Step 4: Mutual Agreement:

Amin ki report ke aadhar par padosi se aapas mein faisla karein.

Step 5: SDM Application:

Agar padosi naapi na manne, toh SDM ke paas Application for Boundary Demarcation lagayein.

🗺️ Bhu Naksha Dekhen ↗
Issue #7

🏗️ Koi mere khet mein ghus aaya hai (Encroachment on Farmland)

Khet mein जबरन ghusna, fasal kaatna ya illegal kheti karna ek dandniya apradh hai.

  • Thanadhyaksha Complaint: Local Police Station mein criminal trespass (IPC Section 447) ki FIR darj karwayein.
  • Anchal Adhikari (CO) Application: CO ko written application dekar Jamabandi aur Possession verify karwayein.
  • Stay Order: SDM court se Section 144/145 ke under ghuspaith par rok lagwayein.
Issue #8

⚖️ Court se Zameen Wapas Chahiye (Civil Court Litigation)

Jab saare aapsi rashte band ho jayein, tab Civil Court se zameen wapas lene ka kanooni tarika:

Title Suit (टाइटिल सूट)

Zameen ke malkana haq (ownership) ko saabit karne aur kabza wapas paane ke liye Civil Judge ke yahan darj hota hai.

Injunction Order (स्टे ऑर्डर)

CPC Rule 39 ke under zameen par kisi bhi prakar ke nirmaan ya bikri par turant rok lagwane ke liye aavedan dein.
