Khata aur Khesra Number Bihar 2026 | खाता और खेसरा नंबर क्या होता है?

💡 Summary Guide 2026

Bihar mein zameen ke do sabse zaroori numbers hote hain:

✅ Khata Number = Aapka Land Account Number (Malik ka khata)

✅ Khesra Number = Aapke Plot ka Number (Survey Plot Number)

Dono ko aap Bihar Bhumi official portal par FREE mein online dekh sakte hain: land.bihar.gov.in/Ror2025/RoR.aspx

📖 Khata Number kya hota hai?

🏦 Bank Account Analogy:
Khata number bilkul aapke bank account number jaisa hota hai — lekin zameen ke liye! Jaise bank mein aapka ek account number hota hai jisme aapke saare paise aur transactions record hote hain, usi tarah zameen ka ek Khata number hota hai jisme aapki us gaon mein saari zameen ka record hota hai.

Khata Number ke Mukhya Points:

  • Ek Gaon mein Ek Khata: Ek zameen malik ke paas ek gaon (Mauja) mein aamtaur par ek hi Khata number hota hai.
  • Kai Plots: Ek Khata number ke andar ek se zyada zameen ke tukde (Khesra / Plots) ho sakte hain.
  • Jamabandi Connection: Khata number Jamabandi Register II se judi hoti hai.
  • Permanence: Yeh number aamtaur par badalta nahi hai jab tak ki naya survey ya legal partition na ho.
  • Bank Loan: KCC ya Home Loan ke liye Khata number maangna compulsory hota hai.

🗺️ Khesra Number kya hota hai?

🏘️ Plot Number Analogy:
Khesra number bilkul plot number jaisa hota hai — jaise kisi residential colony mein har plot ka alag number hota hai! Ek Khata ke andar kai Khesra ho sakte hain, aur har Khesra number ek specific piece of land ko darshata hai.

Khesra Number ke Mukhya Points:

  • Specific Plot: Har physical zameen ke tukde ka ek unique Khesra number hota hai.
  • Multiple Plots: Ek Khate mein 1, 2, 5 ya 10 Khesra numbers ho sakte hain.
  • Survey Assignment: Survey Settlement (जैसे CS, RS या Special Survey) ke dauran yeh number assign hota hai.
  • Boundary & Size: Plot ki exact chauhaddi (boundary), area (रकबा) aur location Khesra se pata chalti hai.
  • Bhu Naksha Map: Khesra number se Bhu Naksha portal par aap apne plot ka map dekh sakte hain.

💡 Vyavaharik Udaharan (Real Example):

Maan lijiye Ramesh Kumar ki aam ke bagicha, khet aur makan teeno ek hi gaon mein hain.

Zameen Malik: Ramesh Kumar

Khata Number: 245 (Ramesh ka ek hi main account)

├── Khesra 456 (Makan wala plot — 5 Katha)

├── Khesra 457 (Khet wala plot — 10 Katha)

└── Khesra 458 (Bagicha wala plot — 3 Katha)

💻 Khata aur Khesra Number Online Kaise Dekhen?

PORTAL 1 — Land Records & RoR

Bihar Bhumi Apna Khata Portal

  1. land.bihar.gov.in/Ror2025/RoR.aspx open karein
  2. Apna District (जिला) select karein
  3. Anchal (प्रखंड/ब्लॉक) select karein
  4. Halka (हल्का) select karein
  5. Mauja (मौजा / गाँव) select karein
  6. Search option chunein:
    • Option A: Khata Number se search
    • Option B: Khesra Number se search
    • Option C: Malik ke Naam se search
  7. Search karein — poora Khata aur Khesra record screen par dikhega
🌐 Khata-Khesra Portal Kholen ↗
PORTAL 2 — Bhu Naksha (Plot Map)

eMapi Bhu Naksha Bihar Portal

  1. emapi.bihar.gov.in open karein
  2. District, Anchal, Halka aur Mauja select karein
  3. Apna Khesra Number enter karein
  4. Search par click karein
  5. Screen par aapke Khesra plot ka exact naksha (map) aur boundaries dikhenge
  6. Map print ya PDF download kar sakte hain

⚡ Khata aur Khesra Number kab kaam aata hai?

Bihar mein zameen se jude lagbhag har official aur sarkari kaam mein in numbers ki zaroorat hoti hai:

🏦

Bank Loan & KCC

LPC certificate aur bank loan (KCC/Home loan) ke liye Khata aur Khesra number compulsory hai.

📋

Dakhil Kharij (Mutation)

Mutation application form mein purana Khata aur Khesra number fill karna compulsory hota hai.

📄

LPC Application

Land Possession Certificate banwane ke liye Khata aur Khesra ka sahi details hona zaroori hai.

🗺️

Bhu Naksha Check

Khesra number se hi aap apni zameen ka naksha (map) dekh aur napwa (survey) sakte hain.

💰

Zameen Bechna / Kharidna

Khariddaar Khata-Khesra number se verify karta hai ki zameen sahi malik ki hai ya nahi.

⚖️

Court & Land Disputes

Zameen vivad ya batwara mein Khata-Khesra number sabse mukhya legal evidence mana jata hai.

❓ Khata ya Khesra Number Nahi Pata?

Agar aapko apni zameen ka Khata ya Khesra number nahi pata, toh in 3 aasaan tareekon se khojein:

👤

Solution 1: Naam se Search

Bihar Bhumi portal par aap Zameen Malik ke Naam se search karke apna Khata number khoj sakte hain.

📄

Solution 2: Purane Kagaz

Purani registry Kewala, Jamabandi rasheed, Lagaan rasheed ya purane LPC par Khata aur Khesra number saaf likha hota hai.

⚠️ Common Problems aur Solutions

Khata aur Khesra search karte waqt aane wali aam samasyaayein aur unka samadhan:

Solution: Spelling check karein. Portals par Hindi / Unicode spelling alag ho sakti hai. Pehle 2-3 akshar se search karein aur sahi Mauja (gaon) hi select karein.
Solution: Agar aapne zameen kharidi hai ya purkhon ki zameen hai, toh Dakhil Kharij (Mutation) ke liye apply karein. Mutation hone par hi naye Khata mein naam judega. Dakhil Kharij Guide dekhein →
Solution: Bihar government ke Parimarjan Plus Portal (parimarjanplus.bihar.gov.in) par Digitized Jamabandi Correction ke liye online application dekar sudhar karwa sakte hain.
Solution: Yeh ho sakta hai agar zameen alag-alag survey (CS aur RS survey) mein ho ya parivar batwara hua ho. Sahi status janne ke liye Anchal office se Register II check karwayein.
Solution: Yeh severe dispute maamla hai. Apne CO / DCLR office mein cancellation ya correction petition file karein ya Bihar Jan Shikayat portal (biharbhumiplus.bihar.gov.in/pg/) par shikayat karein.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Khata number aapka land account number hai. Ek gaon mein ek zameen malik ka ek Khata number hota hai jisme unki sabhi zameenon ka record hota hai.
Khesra number individual plot ka number hota hai. Ek Khate ke andar kai alag-alag Khesra numbers (plots) ho sakte hain.
Khata number malik ka account hota hai jabki Khesra number ek specific zameen ke tukde (plot) ka survey identification number hota hai.
Bihar Bhumi portal (land.bihar.gov.in/Ror2025/RoR.aspx) par District, Anchal, Halka aur Mauja select karke online free mein dekh sakte hain.
Aap Bihar Bhumi portal par Zameen Malik ke Naam se search kar sakte hain ya apne local Anchal office/Halka Karamchari se Sampark kar sakte hain.
Ek Khate mein Khesra number ki koi fixed limit nahi hai. Ek malik ke gaon mein jitne alag plot honge, utne Khesra number us Khate mein honge.
eMapi portal (emapi.bihar.gov.in) par Khesra number enter karke Bhu Naksha online dekh aur download kar sakte hain.
Agricultural loan, KCC ya Home loan ke waqt bank property ki legal ownership aur possession verify karne ke liye Khata aur Khesra number maangta hai.
Normally Khata number nahi badalta. Naye survey (Revisional ya Special Survey) ya batwara hone par naya Khata allot ho sakta hai.
Zameen kharidne ya virasat ke baad Dakhil Kharij (Mutation) ke zariye naya Jamabandi aur Khata add karwaya jaata hai.
Haan, Bihar revenue aur survey system mein Khesra number ko hi plot number ya survey number kaha jata hai.
Bihar government ke Parimarjan Plus portal (parimarjanplus.bihar.gov.in) par online correction application dekar galti sudhar sakte hain.
