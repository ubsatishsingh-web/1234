🛡️ Agent itna kyun maangta hai?

सच्चाई समझें और अपने मेहनत की कमाई बचाएं:

Agents ka koi official government role nahi hota. Woh sirf aapki jagah biharbhumi portal par form fill karte hain aur aap se ₹2,000 se ₹5,000 vasoolte hain.

  • Woh sirf aapki dastawez (Kewala) scanner se scan karke PDF upload karte hain.
  • Yeh kaam aap apne mobile ya computer se 30 minute mein ghar baithe kar sakte hain.
  • Online bihar bhumi portal bilkul free hai — koi hidden charge nahi hai.
  • Agent ko bachakar woh paise aap apne bacho ke education ya zameen ke alag kaam mein use kar sakte hain.

🚫 5 Illegal Charges jo Agents Maangte Hain (Kabhi Mat Dein):

  • ❌ "Speed money" — Bilkul illegal aur gair-kanooni hai.
  • ❌ "File fee" — Aisi koi fee sarkar nahi leti.
  • ❌ "CO sahab ya Revenue Officer ko dena hoga" — Jhooth aur afwah hai.
  • ❌ "Portal fee" — Govt portal 100% FREE hai.
  • ❌ "Extra Government charge" — Sarkari fee sirf ₹100 stamp paper hai.

📝 Agent ke bina Dakhil Kharij kaise karein?

इन 7 आसान स्टेप्स में खुद करें ऑनलाइन आवेदन:

🚀 Dakhil Kharij Portal Kholen →
1

Portal Open Karein

Official website biharbhumi.bihar.gov.in par jayein aur "ऑनलाइन दाखिल-खारिज आवेदन करें" par click karein.

2

Register / Login Karein

Apne mobile number se registration karein aur OTP ke zariye portal mein login karein.

3

Details Fill Karein

District, Circle, Buyer Name, Seller Name, Plot/Khata Number aur Area fill karein.

4

Documents Upload Karein (FREE - ₹0)

Registered Deed (केवाला) ki PDF file scan karke upload karein.

5

Court Fee Stamp Details

₹100 Court Fee Stamp paper ka number aur details enter karein.

6

Final Submit Karein

Form review karke final submit karein. Aapko Application Case Number (वाद संख्या) mil jayega.

7

Status Track Karein

Portal par "दाखिल खारिज आवेदन स्थिति देखें" option se apna status track karein.

⚖️ Kaunse Charges Dene Chahiye?

सही और गलत खर्चे का फर्क जानें:

✅ Valid & Legal Charges:

  • ✓ Court fee stamp: ₹100
  • ✓ Document photocopy: ₹20 - ₹50
  • ✓ Travel to CO office (if required)
  • ✓ Lawyer fee (if complex case): ₹1000+

❌ Invalid & Illegal Charges:

  • ✕ Agent / Dalal fee: DON'T PAY
  • ✕ Speed money to office staff: ILLEGAL
  • ✕ Portal / filing fee: DOESN'T EXIST
  • ✕ Any payment to middleman: AVOID
