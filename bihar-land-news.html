CIRCLE RATE July 2026

"Bihar Mein Circle Rate 2026 — Kya Badla? Patna Sabse Zyada Mehanga"

Bihar sarkar ne 2026 mein circle rate mein kuch badlav kiye hain. Patna city ke core areas mein rate sabse zyada hain — ₹8 lakh se ₹25 lakh per katha tak. Gramin ilaqon mein rate stable hai...

Naye circle rate se stamp duty ka calculation bhi badal gaya hai. Zameen kharidne waalon ko naye rates ke hisaab se budget banana chahiye. Bihta, Danapur aur Phulwarisharif jaise semi-urban regions mein land registries par 15% to 20% tak ka rate revision dekhne ko mila hai.

Key Takeaway for Buyers:

  • Patna Bailey Road and Boring Road core area rates touched ₹25 Lakh+ per Katha.
  • Gramin and diara lands have kept identical rates to prevent artificial inflation.
  • Ensure checking the official Minimum Valuation Register (MVR) before booking land token.

GOVERNMENT SCHEME July 2026

"PM Awas Yojana Bihar 2026 — Nayi List Mein Apna Naam Kaise Dekhen"

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin (PMAY-G) ke tehat Bihar ke gramin kshetron mein naye labharthiyon ki list 2026 jaari ho gayi hai. Bihar rural department ne digital list publish ki hai...

Apna naam check karne ke liye official PMAY-G portal par jaakar state (Bihar), district, block aur panchayat select karein. Is baar labharthiyon ko ₹1.20 Lakh ki seedhi rashi subsidy ke roop mein di jayegi. Naye niyam ke anusar, bank khata direct benefit transfer (DBT) ke liye aadhar-seeded hona compulsory hai.

"Dakhil Kharij Bihar — Naya Portal Update Aur Nayi Process 2026"

Bihar revenue department ne online Dakhil Kharij (Land Mutation) portal ko bilkul naye software infrastructure par launch kiya hai. Isme system automated FIFO rule implement karega...
