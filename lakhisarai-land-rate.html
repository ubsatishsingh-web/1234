Lakhisarai District Ke Baare Mein

Lakhisarai district Bihar ka ek sanskritik aur dharmik drishti se atyant mahatvapurna zila hai. Yeh shahar prashiddh "Ashok Dham" mandir (jo ki Bhagwan Shiva ka ek mahan dharmik sthal hai) ke liye poore desh mein lokpriya hai. Ganga nadi aur Kiul nadi ke sangam ke nikat hone ke karan is jile ki mitti behad upjau hai, jiski wajah se kheti ki zameen ki kafi unchi moolyankan dar bani rehti hai.

Patna se Bhagalpur ko jodne wali National Highway 80 (NH 80) is jile ki real estate market ke liye vardaan sabit hui hai. Lakhisarai City aur Ashok Dham road ke nikat plots ki demand lagatar badh rahi hai aur yahan ki zameen ke bhav majboot up-trend dikha rahe hain. Surya-garha aur Halsi jaise badhte urban-pockets mein bhi residential zameeno ke rates badh rahe hain. Yeh zila dharmik paryatan, behtar connectivity aur agricultural growth ke sath real estate buyers ke liye ek vishwasniya market pradan karta hai.

A: Surajgarha mein zameen ka bhav ₹20,000 se ₹70,000 per katha aur Halsi sector mein ₹15,000 se ₹55,000 per katha tak chal raha hai.
