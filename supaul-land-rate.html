Supaul District Ke Baare Mein

Supaul, Bihar ke Kosi division ka ek atyant mukhya zila hai jo Kosi nadi ke vishal kachhar (river basin) mein sthit hai. Uttar mein yah zila Nepal desh ke sath apni lambi antarrashtriya sima sajha karta hai. Kosi Barrage, jo ki Birpur block mein sthit hai, is zile ki ek behad mukhya pehchan aur paryatan sthal hai. Supaul ki mitti Kosi nadi ke behad upjau alluvial mitti se bani hai, jo makhana aur dhaan ki kheti ke liye pure vishwa mein anukul mani jati hai. Halanki, yah kshetr badh aur pani ke jamao (seasonal inundations) se prabhavit hota hai, isliye yahan real estate investment mein khas savdhani barati jati hai.

Supaul shahar ke surakshit aur unche kshetron jaise main town, Pipra road aur Station road par zameen ka rate ₹25,000 se ₹1,20,000 per katha tak chal raha hai. Birpur aur border crossway areas ke mukhya bazaar kshetron mein plot rates ₹20,000 se ₹90,000 per katha residential plots ke liye aur commercial spots par ₹35,000 se ₹1,40,000 per katha tak hain. Kosi flood plains (baadh kshetra) mein sthit zameen ke rates aam taur par kafi kam hote hain aur wahan kharidari karne se pehle sthaniy log hamesha water level histories check karte hain, taaki monsoon ke dino mein kisi nuksan se bacha ja sake.
